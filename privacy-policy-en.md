# Privacy Policy

**Nyong** app ("App") values your privacy and complies with applicable data protection laws.

**Last updated**: February 7, 2026

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide our service:

| Data | Purpose | Retention |
|------|---------|-----------|
| Email address | Account identification and login | Until account deletion |
| Nickname | Display name in the app | Until account deletion |
| Push notification token | Sending cat photo notifications | Until account deletion |
| Uploaded photos | Sharing with other users | Until deletion request |
| Notification exclusion times | Applying do-not-disturb settings | Until account deletion |

## 2. How We Use Your Information

Collected information is used only for:

- Account registration and login services
- Sending cat photo push notifications once daily at random times
- Delivering cat photos uploaded by users to other users
- Applying notification exclusion time settings
- Service improvement and analytics

## 3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties.

Exceptions:
- When required by law
- When you have given prior consent

## 4. Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored encrypted on [Supabase](https://supabase.com) cloud service
- We use secure HTTPS communication
- Access is restricted to your own data (Row Level Security)

## 5. Your Rights

You can exercise the following rights at any time:

- **Access**: View your information in Settings
- **Modify**: Change nickname and notification settings
- **Delete**: Request deletion of all data via Settings > Delete Account

## 6. Cookies and Tracking

The app does not use cookies. However, we securely store session tokens on your device to maintain login status.

## 7. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 14. If we become aware that a child under 14 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

## 8. Advertising

The app may include rewarded advertisements. Ad networks (Google AdMob) may collect device identifiers for ad delivery.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we will notify you through in-app announcements or push notifications.
